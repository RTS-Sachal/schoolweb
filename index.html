About Us

Roshan Tara Secondary School was established in 1993 with the vision to provide quality education, strong moral values, and a disciplined learning environment for the children of Sachal Goth, Karachi.

SMC Chairman Mr Taj Muhammad Wassan

Our mission at Roshan Tara Secondary School is to nurture disciplined, confident, and knowledgeable students who can contribute positively to society and the nation.

SMC Vice Chairman Mr Sahib Dino Arbab

The Vice Chairman supports the Chairman in strategic decisions and ensures the school's policies and initiatives are effectively implemented.

SMC Educationist Mr.Ayaz Hussain Wassan

The Educationist provides guidance on curriculum development and innovative teaching practices, ensuring quality education for all students.
